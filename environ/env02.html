On a dark and cold night...

the streets of New Meridian fester with the ubiquitous prescence of crime.

with the dawn of the new Skullgirl, the residents of this city constantly live in fear for their lives. There seems to be no hope.
They need a hero. One with the heart of a beast.

there's only one man who can save us now...

THE WULF.

weighing in at 287 pounds of folded steel, he shows no mercy to evildoers, in and out the ring.
he fights for justice, for he is justice.
